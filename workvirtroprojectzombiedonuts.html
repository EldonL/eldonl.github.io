Zombie Donuts Using your marshmallow blaster, destroy the zombie donuts coming from around you. You can get bonus points by hitting bonus candies and trick shots. Compete with your friends to be at the top of the leaderboard Oculus Go C# Unity Nov -Dec 2018, Dec 2020
Making updates to an existing app that is already published on the Oculus Store. There was another update in 2020 where the Oculus GO was being deprecated, so we made final updates before the GO was going to stop being supported but users can still buy our game There were 2 updates. One in 2018 where I was tasked to add bonus points if the user hits bonus candies. Another in 2020, where I had to check if the leaderboards was still functioning and upload to the Oculus Store When the candy was hit, I got to implement respawning the candy in the same location. If the candy was hit, "x" amount of score would be added to the total score. The candy would be destroyed and get respawned The leaderboard also needed to be updated, my job was to make sure the leaderboard was connected to Oculus. I did this by checking the platform services - leaderboards was active I helped managed the publishing process and the app was republished to the store As a fun little fact, when you shoot at the title in the game, you can see who worked on the app. Below shows my name as proof I worked on this game
