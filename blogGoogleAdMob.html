Depending on if I can sideload or not, I may or may not purchase this While I wait. Let's see what types of Google AdMob ads there are based on the Get Started Documentation. First one is Banner ads. According to the documentation "rectangular image or text ads that occupy a spot on screen. They stay on screen while users are interacting with the app, and can refresh automatically after a certain period of time". Next is the Interstitial ads. According to the documentation, these "are full-screen ads that cover the interface of their host app. They're typically displayed at natural transition points in the flow of an app, such as between activities or during the pause between levels in a game. When an app shows an interstitial ad, the user has the choice to either tap on the ad and continue to its destination or close it and return to the app." Next is the native ads. "Native ads are ad assets that are presented to users through UI components that are native to the platform. They're shown using the same types of views with which you're already building your layouts, and can be formatted to match your app's visual design" Lastly, my favourite one, rewards ads These ads "reward users for watching short videos and interacting with playable ads and surveys. Used for monetizing free-to-play apps" which is used in games like "Battle Run" and "Tap Titans 2". For example, in Battle Run after you finish a game, you can press a button called "Bonus Rewards" watch an add to get coins Tap titans 2 has reward ads when you go back into the game. They will have a "Welcome back" message and if you press the "Extra rewards" button, it let you watch ads to get the rewards Tap titans 2 also has reward ads during the game. There will be fairy carrying a treasure chest, when you tap on the fairy, a window will appear asking if you want to watch ads to get rewards like coins or gems or power ups It's been 7 hours and the aab is still building.
